## 2. Fair Use Policy
GisunOS utilizes snippets, thumbnails, and metadata from third-party services. We believe this usage constitutes **Fair Use** under international copyright laws (including Section 107 of the U.S. Copyright Act) for the following reasons:

*   **Transformative Nature**: The content is integrated into a unique, simulated operating system environment, providing a new context and experience separate from the original source.
*   **Non-Commercial Purpose**: The project is entirely free, open-source, and intended for educational and demonstrative use only.
*   **Market Impact**: GisunOS does not compete with the original services; rather, it directs users back to the original platforms (e.g., YouTube) via official APIs.
*   **Portion Used**: Only necessary metadata (titles, thumbnails, IDs) is retrieved to facilitate embedding via official, authorized third-party players.
